What Linh has learned from a Liberal Arts College?

Studying at Luther has trained Linh to work under pressure and improve her time management skills. The amount of assignments and projects that each class has given her the chance to learn how to balance between studying, working, and participating extra-curricular activities.

Also, she has learned a positive attitude to keep learning new knowledge. Studying in a Liberal Arts College with a huge variety of courses, Linh has access to fields that she has not known before such as religions, philosophy, psychology, etc. This has enabled her to grow as a human and open her mind, creating an open-minded and knowledge thriven person.

Studying in a small college has improved Linh's communication and leadership skills. She has more chance to interact with professors and her classmates as well as get more involve in school activities. These chances have taught Linh responsibilities and let her know that she wants to be in the leadership roles more to help her school communities. Also, studying in a small school offers Linh opportunities to get to know the community better such as what the students want, what they like and dislike, etc. ; therefore, she could serve her communities better.

Linh has learned and been given a lot of opportunities at Luther College. She is graceful to be a part of the community and she will try her best to make it a great place.
